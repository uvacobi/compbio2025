\[\begin{aligned} T(n) & = T(n-1) + T(n-2) + 1 \\ & < 2 \times T(n-1) + 1 \\ & < 4 \times T(n-2) + 3 \\ & \ldots \\ & < 2^k \times T(n-k) + (2^k - 1) \\ & = \ldots \\ & < 2^n \times T(0) + (2^n - 1) \\ & = 2^n + 2^n - 1 \\ & = O(2^n) \end{aligned} \]
Note: In reality the time complexity of this recurrence is O(1.618^n) --- ## Computing Fibonacci numbers ```python def fibonacci(n): fib_table = {} fib_table[1] = 1 fib_table[2] = 1 for i in range(3,n+1): fib_table[i] = fib_table[i-1] + fib_table[i-2] return fib_table[n] ``` ![F7](images/alignments/f7_memoize.png) Note: What is the time complexity here? O(n). Burrows-Wheeler transform * Initially developed for compression * Reversible permutation of the characters of a string ![BTW](images/alignments/BWT.png) --- ## Burrows-Wheeler transform ```code def rotate(sequence, n): return sequence[n:] + sequence[:n] def BWT(sequence): seq = sequence + "$" perms = [rotate(seq,i) for i in range(len(seq))] return "".join([x[-1] for x in sorted(perms)]) ``` ```text >>> string = "abaaba" >>> BWT(string) 'abba$aa' >>> string = "Let_me_tell_you_a_little_story_all_about_the_DNA" >>> BWT(string) 'AN_$Deulyateetl___ahlmtLtlllt_ae_tybo_eu__tsioor_' ``` --- ## Insight: BWT provides "structure" $BWT(T)$ orders the characters of $T$ according to alphabetical order of their right context (characters that come after it) ![BWT_insight](images/alignments/BWT_insight.png) --- ##
